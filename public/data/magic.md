# MAGIC Framework
**Managed AI Governance & Implementation Controls**

MAGIC is a controls framework for MSPs delivering AI across customer environments. It defines what to govern, what to secure, and how to operate AI as a repeatable managed service.

Every control maps to one motion: **Govern. Secure. Operate.**

---

## Govern
Establish ownership and boundaries before AI spreads. Who owns it, what's in scope, how changes get approved.

## Secure
Make AI respect identity, data, and environment boundaries. Enforce policy — don't just document it.

## Operate
Run AI like a production service. Deploy in stages, monitor for drift, and have a rollback plan. If you can't operate it, don't enable it.

---

## What belongs in the framework

A control must be **operational** (something you can actually do), have a **clear owner**, produce **evidence**, **scale across tenants**, and map to GSO. If it fails any of those, it doesn't belong.

Not in scope: vendor rankings, prompt tips, ethics without enforcement, one-time assessments.
